Women's Rights

Why It Is Important

Women are misjudged worldwide and mistreated by others. Women had less educational privileges which gives them less knowlege of things, which gives them the ability to cook and clean for family and men. Women are stereotyped to be housewives that do not have a strong, educational workplace.

How Is Women Rights and Men Rights Differ

  • 130 million girls are out of school
  • Every 5 minutes, a girl dies as a result of violence
  • 71% of human trafficking victims are female
  • 63 million girls have undergone female genital mutilation
  • One in four girls gets married as a child worldwide
  • Girls are twice as likely to become infected with HIV versus men

Girls, Not Brides

  • Each year, 15 million girls are married before the age of 18. That is 28 girls every minute, and 1 every 2 seconds
  • Child marriage is any formal marriage or informal union where one or both of the parties are under 18 years of age
  • Child marriage is rooted in gender inequality and the belief that girls and women are somehow inferior to boys and men
  • Poverty, lack of education, cultural practices, and insecurity fuel and sustain the practice
  • Girls are not valued as much as boys - they are seen as a burden on their family
